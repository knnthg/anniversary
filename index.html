My Dearest Love,

Happy anniversary to the most incredible woman I could ever dream of. As I sit and think about our time together, my heart feels so full it could burst, and it’s all because of you.

I was thinking today about the countless ways you show your love, and it always comes back to one thing: you constantly pour your entire heart into making me happy. I am in awe of you.

I think of you in the kitchen, creating magic not just with ingredients, but with love. Every meal you’ve ever cooked for me is so much more than food. It’s your time, your effort, and your desire to nurture me and see me smile. I taste your love in every single bite, and I hope you know it’s my favorite flavor in the entire world.

And my darling, I think of your generosity, and it truly humbles me. Even when you’re running low, your first thought is always, “What can I do for you?” or “What can I get for you?” You have this magical way of finding joy in my joy. The things you’ve bought me are wonderful, but they are just things. The real gift, the one that leaves me speechless, is the selfless, beautiful heart behind it. You give so freely, not because you have a lot, but because you are a lot, so much love, so much kindness, so much care. Please never feel you have to do that, your love is more than enough.

You have built our happiness brick by brick with your acts of service, your unwavering support, and your incredible, big heart. You make me feel so cherished, so seen, and so deeply loved every single day.

Which is why, with this full and grateful heart, I also need to say I’m sorry. I’m so sorry that we get caught up in arguments over the smallest things. Arguing with you feels wrong because you are the last person in the world I ever want to be at odds with. You are my safe place, my joy, and my home. I hate that our stubbornness sometimes clouds the beautiful connection we have. I am truly committed to doing better, to listening more, and to choosing our peace over being right.

Thank you for being my partner, my best friend, and my soulmate. I promise to cherish you, support you, and love you with all my heart for all the days of my life.

Thank you for loving me the way you do. Thank you for every meal, every sacrifice, every thoughtful gift, and for a love that is so active and generous. I am the luckiest person alive to be loved by you.

Happy Anniversary, my love. Here’s to us, and to many more years of learning, growing, and loving each other, even through our imperfections. I love you more than words can say.

Forever yours,
Kenneth
